Swedish Egg Sandwich

Description

A traditional swedish snack

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Eggs, boiled
  • Kalles kaviar
  • Chives
  • Pepper
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  1. Put butter on a piece of bread.
  2. Chop eggs into thin slices.
  3. Put eggs on bread.
  4. Put kaviar on top of eggs in a wavy pattern.
  5. Sprinkle some shopped chives.
  6. For some extra heat, add chili flakes.
  7. Add pepper to taste.
