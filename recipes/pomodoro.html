Pomodoro

Description

A simple and delicious dish from Italy

Ingredients:

  • Garlic
  • Pasta
  • Pomodoro tomatoes
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Basil
  • Unsalted butter
  • White wine
  • Salt to taste
  1. Add water to a pot and bring to boil.
  2. Add plenty of salt to the water.
  3. Add the pasta and cook until done.
  4. Strain the pasta. Save some of the water for later.
  5. Add olive oil to a pan over medium heat.
  6. Fry the tomatoes and garlic
  7. Add butter, white wine, and some of the pasta water
  8. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  9. Plate, and garnish with basil.
